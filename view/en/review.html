Information for reviewers

Dear Reviewer!

We would like to ask you to use special form for reviewing materials presented for reviewing by Editorial board.

Download:

  • Special form for reviewing materials (pdf).
  • Special form for reviewing materials (doc).

If you are agree to give a review on the material, we ask you to send a review to Editorial board during 20 days from the moment of receiving by you copy of manuscript in an electronic or hard variant.

In the case of possible delaying of reviewing we ask you to inform Editorial board about it via e-mail or regular mail. In the case of some questions, wishes or remarks please contact Editorial board.

Please, do not forget, the procedure of reviewing is anonymous!
